Pepperoni Pizza Recipie

Description

This lovely recipie is for a pepperoni pizza. It is made with pepperoni, mozzerella cheese, tomato sauce, and of course, the dough. This dish brings the family together lol

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces pizza dough, store-bought or homemade (I like this no-knead version, though note that this recipe makes enough dough for 2 pizzas)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 18 to 20 slices pepperoni
  • 12 ounces mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon fresh oregano, optional
  • Flour for rolling and shaping dough

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 500 degrees Farenheight
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Roll out the dough
  4. Add the toppings
  5. Cook the pizza
  6. Slice and serve
